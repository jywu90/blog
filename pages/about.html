About Me

Jingyao Wu is a normal human being who currently resides on earth. To be specific, Pittsburgh, a lovely mid-size city, located in southwest Pennsylvania, USA. Back in 1990, Jingyao was born in a beautiful small town along Yangtze river in Southeast China, and enjoyed his whole childhood there before he left for college in 2007.

Believing in a future where science and technology will truly make the world a better place, Jingyao started his science journey in college by learning the magic of gluing atoms and chemical groups together, which muggles call Chemistry. Driven by the enthusiasm in levergae his knowlege to solve real world problem, he moved to Pittsburgh in summer 2012 and started his graduate school at University of Pittsburgh, Department of Bioengineering. This fine place introduced him to helpful people, brilliant ideas and incredible tech community which shape the person he is today. Jingyao is working on developing new engineering solutions that revolutionize the way we treat human disease, which is generally called biomedical engineering. His passion roots in the belief that, one day in the future, these technologies will be able to make strong the week, mend the broken and bring the smile back on patients' faces.

For most of the time, he is a quiet person, you will either find him in a research building called Benedum Hall working or an apartment called morrowfield sleeping. Apart from that, he likes to chill with friends, try his hand at an complex recipe, challenge himself with outdoor advendure or just stay at home palying PC games whole day. Surprise him with new things, as a curious person, he is always ready to jump in new stuff, learning and sharing.
